Hi, I am Michael, and I love puns a little too much. I want to put my love of puns and my illustration skills to good use (well to use anyway) by making shirts. You can visit my store on Threadless to see my very fist design.

Why the name, Puritan Designs

The idea for a fictitious design company came to mind as a Puritan spinoff of the classic phrase, "We spare no expense."

The Puritans tend to have a prudish reputation. Of course they would spare every expense. Well, it was funny at the time, and it stuck with me. Yet another play on words.
