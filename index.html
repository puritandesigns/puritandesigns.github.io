Why the name, Puritan Designs

The idea for a fictitious design company came to mind as a Puritan spinoff of the classic phrase, "We spare no expense."

The Puritans tend to have a prudish reputation. Of course they would spare every expense. Well, it was funny at the time, and it stuck with me. So now I use it as my moniker.
